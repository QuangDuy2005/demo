Low and Slow

Posted by BGB, November 15, 2016

This week I am extremely exvited about a new cooking technique calledsous vide. In sous videcooking, you submerge the food(usually
vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to. In his book,
Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Potter describe it as ultra-low-temperature poaching.
